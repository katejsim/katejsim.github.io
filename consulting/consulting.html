Consulting

I frequently work with civil society, government, and industry groups to advise them on a range of topics at the intersection of child safety, privacy, and tech policy.

Typically, this incluldes: providing a landscape review of a particular topic; writing and reviewing policy language; developing trauma-informed language and flow for resources (i.e., user reporting form); identifying and convening relevant stakeholders; and developing messaging or campaign strategies, among others.

If you'd like to work with me, please contact me at katesimLLC@pm.me with a brief description of who you are, what you do, and what problems you are facing.
