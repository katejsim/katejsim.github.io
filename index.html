Kate J. Sim

(she/her)

I am an expert researcher, advisor, and convener who specializes in the intersection of child safety, sexual violence, and tech policy.

I have over 11 years of experience in advocacy and organizing, research, and policymaking across academia, government, and industry. I bring this depth to my current role directing a program on Children's Online Safety and Privacy Research (COSPR) at UWA Law - Tech & Policy Lab. You can read more about the program here.

I have a PhD and MSc from the Oxford Internet Institute and a BA in gender and sexuality studies from Harvard.
